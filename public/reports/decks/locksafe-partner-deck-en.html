02 — The problem
Where the market fails

An emergency category, built on guesswork.

Locksmith service is one of the most stressful, time-sensitive purchases a customer can make. Today it runs on opaque pricing, fragmented supply, and no proof of work — a structural trust deficit that hurts both sides.

Customer side

Panic without proof

  • Hidden pricing and bait-and-switch quotes.
  • No verifiable evidence of arrival, work, or condition.
  • Disputes are word-against-word; resolution is slow.
Provider side

Cost without certainty

  • Lead-fee platforms charge on intent, not outcome.
  • No-shows and dead-end calls erode margin daily.
  • Payouts are slow, opaque, and disconnected from quality.
