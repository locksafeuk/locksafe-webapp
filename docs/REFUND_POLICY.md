# LockSafe UK - Refund Policy

> **Last Updated: 4 March 2026**

---

## Overview

At LockSafe UK, we believe in fair treatment for both customers and locksmiths. Our refund policy is designed to protect customers while respecting the time and effort of our locksmith partners.

---

## Customer Refund Rights

### When You Can Get a Refund

#### 1. Locksmith No-Show
**Full Refund of Assessment Fee**

If the locksmith you booked:
- Does not arrive at all, OR
- Arrives more than 30 minutes after their stated ETA

You are entitled to a **full refund** of your Assessment Fee.

**How to claim:**
1. Open your job in the LockSafe app/website
2. Click "Request Refund"
3. Select "Locksmith didn't arrive"
4. We verify using GPS data
5. Refund processed automatically

**Timeline:** 3-5 business days to appear in your account

---

#### 2. Booking Cancellation (Before Locksmith Dispatch)
**Full Refund of Assessment Fee**

If you cancel your booking **before** the locksmith has been dispatched:
- You receive a full refund
- No cancellation fee applies

**Important:** Once the locksmith has left to travel to your location, the Assessment Fee is non-refundable (see below).

---

### When You Cannot Get a Refund

#### Assessment Fee After Locksmith Travels
**Non-Refundable**

The Assessment Fee compensates the locksmith for:
- Their travel time
- Their fuel/transport costs
- Their diagnostic expertise

Once the locksmith has been dispatched and travels to your location, the Assessment Fee is **non-refundable** even if:
- You change your mind
- You're not home
- You decline the Work Quote

**Why?** The locksmith has already invested their time and money to come to you. This policy ensures locksmiths are fairly compensated and willing to take jobs.

---

#### Work Already Completed
**Non-Refundable**

Once you have:
- Accepted a Work Quote
- Had the work completed
- Signed the digital confirmation

The payment is **non-refundable**. Your signature confirms satisfaction with the work.

**If there's a problem with the work:**
- Contact the locksmith directly
- If unresolved, contact us for dispute mediation
- We review GPS, photos, and signature evidence

---

## Locksmith Refund Responsibilities

### Refund Deduction From Your Account

If a refund is issued to a customer due to:
- Your no-show
- Your late arrival (30+ minutes past ETA)

The refund amount will be **debited from your Stripe Connect account**.

### Avoiding Refunds

✓ Set realistic ETAs
✓ Allow buffer time for traffic
✓ Communicate delays to the customer ASAP
✓ If you can't make it, cancel early (before travelling)

### Excessive Refunds

Locksmiths with a pattern of refunds may face:
- Account review
- Temporary suspension
- Permanent removal from the platform

## Dispute Resolution

### Step 1: Contact Us
Email: support@locksafe.uk
Subject: "Dispute - Job #[JOB_ID]"

### Step 2: Evidence Review
We review:
- GPS arrival/completion data
- Timestamped photos
- Digital signature (if applicable)
- Communication records

### Step 3: Decision
- We make a fair decision based on evidence
- Decision communicated within 5 business days
- Either party can appeal once

### Step 4: Appeals
If you disagree with our decision:
- Submit appeal within 14 days
- Provide any additional evidence
- Final decision within 10 business days

## Automatic Refund Triggers

These trigger an automatic refund without manual review:

| Trigger | Refund |
|---------|--------|
| Locksmith marks "cannot attend" | 100% Assessment Fee |
| No GPS arrival within ETA + 30 min | 100% Assessment Fee |
| Customer cancels before dispatch | 100% Assessment Fee |
| Payment processing error | 100% of amount charged |

## Payment Methods

### Refund to Original Payment Method
- All refunds are processed to the original payment method
- Credit/debit cards: 3-5 business days
- Bank transfers: 5-7 business days

### Failed Refund Attempts
If the refund cannot be processed (e.g., card expired):
- We will contact you
- Alternative refund method arranged
- May include bank transfer or credit on account

## Special Circumstances

### Emergency Situations
If you need to cancel due to:
- Medical emergency
- Family emergency
- Property emergency

Contact us immediately. We handle each case individually and may offer goodwill refunds.

### Technical Issues
If our platform experiences technical issues that affect your booking:
- Full refund provided
- Plus any reasonable compensation

### Fraud
Any attempt to fraudulently obtain refunds will result in:
- Account termination
- Legal action if appropriate
- Reporting to relevant authorities
