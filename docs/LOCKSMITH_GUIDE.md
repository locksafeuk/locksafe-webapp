## How to Register

### Step 1: Apply Online
Visit [locksafe.uk/locksmith-signup](https://locksafe.uk/locksmith-signup) and fill out the application form:
- Your name and contact details
- Company name (optional)
- Years of experience
- Coverage areas/postcodes
- Services you offer

### Step 2: Verification
We verify all locksmiths to ensure quality:
- ✓ ID verification
- ✓ Proof of qualifications
- ✓ Insurance documentation (public liability)
- ✓ DBS background check

### Step 3: Set Up Stripe Connect
To receive payments, you'll need to:
1. Complete Stripe Connect onboarding
2. Link your bank account
3. Verify your identity with Stripe

This takes about 5-10 minutes and is completely free.

### Step 4: Set Your Coverage Area
In your dashboard:
1. Set your base location (postcode)
2. Set your coverage radius (e.g., 15 miles)
3. Add specific areas you serve

### Step 5: Go Live!
Once verified, you'll see available jobs in your area and can start applying.

## How the Platform Works

### The Customer Journey
1. Customer requests a locksmith on our website
2. They describe their problem and location
3. Verified locksmiths in the area see the job
4. Locksmiths apply with their **Assessment Fee** and **ETA**
5. Customer chooses a locksmith based on price, rating, and ETA
6. Customer pays the Assessment Fee upfront
7. You travel to the job and complete the work

### Your Dashboard
Your locksmith dashboard shows:
- **Available Jobs** - New jobs in your coverage area
- **My Jobs** - Active jobs you've been assigned
- **Earnings** - Your total earnings and pending payouts
- **Reviews** - Customer feedback on your work
- **Settings** - Profile, coverage area, and preferences

## Your Earnings & Commission

### How Pricing Works

| Component | Who Sets It | Example |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| **Assessment Fee** | You | £49 - £89 |
| **Work Quote** | You | Based on job requirements |
| **Platform Commission** | LockSafe | 15% of total |
| **Your Earnings** | What you keep | 85% of total |

### Example Job

```
Assessment Fee:     £59.00 (You keep 85% = £50.15)
Work Quote:        £120.00 (You keep 75% = £90.00)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────
Total Job Value:   £179.00

Platform Fee:       £38.85 (21.7%)
Your Earnings:     £140.15 (78.3%) ✓
```

### No Hidden Fees
- ✗ No monthly subscription
- ✗ No sign-up fee
- ✗ No minimum jobs
- ✗ No lead fees
- ✓ Tiered commission on completed jobs only

### Why Tiered Commission?

| Fee Type | Why Lower/Higher Commission |
|----------|----------------------------|
| **Assessment Fee (15%)** | You set your own price, we just help match you |
| **Work Quote (25%)** | Covers payment processing, fraud protection, GPS tracking, PDF reports, customer support |

## Getting Paid

### Stripe Connect
All payments are processed through **Stripe Connect**, the world's leading payment platform used by companies like Deliveroo, Uber, and Amazon.

### Payout Schedule
- **Standard payouts**: 1-2 business days after job completion
- **Express payouts**: Available after account verification (small fee may apply)

### How It Works
1. Customer pays → Funds held by Stripe
2. Customer signs confirmation → Payment released
3. Your earnings transferred to your Stripe account
   - 85% of assessment fee
   - 75% of work quote
4. Stripe sends to your bank → Money in your account!

### Tracking Your Earnings
In your dashboard, you can see:
- **Available Balance** - Ready for payout
- **Pending Balance** - Jobs awaiting customer confirmation
- **Total Earned** - Your lifetime earnings
- **Payout History** - All past payouts with dates

## Job Types & Workflow

### Step-by-Step Job Flow

#### 1. **Job Alert**
- You receive a notification of a new job
- See: Location, problem description, urgency level

#### 2. **Apply**
- Set your Assessment Fee (£49-£99 typical)
- Set your ETA (how soon you can arrive)
- Customer sees your profile, rating, and price

#### 3. **Win the Job**
- Customer selects you
- They pay the Assessment Fee
- You receive confirmation and address

#### 4. **Travel & Arrive**
- GPS check-in when you arrive
- "Before" photo of the lock/problem
- Meet the customer

#### 5. **Diagnose & Quote**
- Assess the problem
- Create a detailed Work Quote
- Customer can accept or decline

#### 6. **Complete the Work**
- Fix the problem
- Take "after" photos
- Document parts used

#### 7. **Get Signed Off**
- Customer signs on your device
- Digital signature = job complete
- Payment released automatically

#### 8. **Get Paid!**
- Your 85% is transferred
- Customer receives PDF report
- Review request sent to customer

### What If Customer Declines the Quote?
No problem! The customer has the right to decline your Work Quote. In this case:
- You keep the Assessment Fee (for your time and travel)
- Job closes with no further obligation
- Both parties protected

## FAQ

### Registration & Account

**Q: Is there a sign-up fee?**
A: No, registration is completely free.

**Q: How long does verification take?**
A: Usually 1-3 business days once all documents are submitted.

**Q: Can I work for other platforms?**
A: Yes, LockSafe is non-exclusive. Work wherever you like.

**Q: Do I need insurance?**
A: Yes, public liability insurance is required for verification.

### Jobs & Earnings

**Q: How do I get more jobs?**
A: Keep your rating high, respond quickly to job alerts, and set competitive prices.

**Q: What if I can't make it to a job?**
A: Contact the customer ASAP and mark the job as cancelled. Repeated cancellations affect your rating.

**Q: When do I get paid?**
A: Payouts typically arrive 1-2 business days after customer signature.

**Q: What's the minimum payout?**
A: No minimum - we transfer whatever you've earned.

### Platform Questions

**Q: How does GPS tracking work?**
A: When you mark "arrived", we capture your GPS location. This proves you attended the job.

**Q: What if there's a dispute?**
A: We review the GPS data, photos, and digital signature to resolve fairly.

**Q: How do reviews work?**
A: Customers can leave a review after job completion. Only verified jobs can be reviewed.
